Who We Are

An Integrated Partner Across Nigeria's Critical Industries

Priziangori Global Services Limited is a multi-sector services company headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, built to deliver dependable, compliant, and sustainable solutions to clients operating across Nigeria's most demanding industries.

From upstream oil and gas support to environmental remediation, haulage, engineering contracts, cross-border trade, and hospitality, our teams combine local insight with international standards of execution — giving clients a single, accountable partner for complex, multi-disciplinary work.

Oil & Gas Services

Oil & Gas Services

Environmental & Waste Management

Environmental & Waste Management

As a responsible environmental service company, we offer sustainable solutions such as waste management, oil spill cleanup, pollution control, land reclamation, sand filling, and shore protection. Logistics, Haulage & Procurement

Reliable haulage, fleet management, and end-to-end procurement and supply chain solutions for industrial and commercial clients.

Logistics, Haulage & Procurement

Priziangori Global Services Limited operates a reliable haulage and logistics service in Port Harcourt, providing efficient supply chain management, equipment leasing, procurement, and manpower supply. Our logistics division ensures your goods, tools, and equipment reach their destination safely and on time.

Engineering & Contracting

Civil works, site engineering, and contracting services delivered with strict attention to safety, quality, and project timelines.

Engineering & Contracting

Import, Export & General Trade

Import, Export & General Trade

Hospitality & Tourism
