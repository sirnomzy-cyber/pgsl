Who We Are

An Integrated Partner Across Nigeria's Critical Industries

Priziangori Global Services Limited is a multi-sector services company headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, built to deliver dependable, compliant, and sustainable solutions to clients operating across Nigeria's most demanding industries.

From upstream oil and gas support to environmental remediation, haulage, engineering contracts, cross-border trade, and hospitality, our teams combine local insight with international standards of execution — giving clients a single, accountable partner for complex, multi-disciplinary work.
